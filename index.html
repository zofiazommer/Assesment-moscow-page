All four seasons can be enjoyed in Russian but probably th ebest time to go visit is during summer as it is peak tourist season and the best time as it has ideal temperatures for spending time there.

Some areas of departures allow the flight to Russia (Moscow) but if possible it's better to go there by train or bus as there is a conflict between Russia and Ukraine that impacted the traveling methods and it is best to be careful as there is the risk of terrorism.
